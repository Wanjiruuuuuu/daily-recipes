Tumeric Black Pepper Chicken With Broccoli

This is a vibrant, flavorful dish that combines the warmth of turmeric with the kick of black pepper. The sweet and peppery taste is perfectly balanced, making it a delicious, quick stir-fry for busy weeknights.

INGREDIENTS

  • 1/4 cup water
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 3/4 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons ground turmeric
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 pound skinless, boneless chicken thighs, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1/2 pound broccoli, cut into small florets
  • 1 (5 ounce) can water chestnuts, drained
  • 1 teaspoon rice vinegar

STEPS

  1. Whisk water, honey, and pepper together in a small bowl. Set aside.
  2. Whisk flour, turmeric, and salt together in a separate bowl.
  3. Heat oil in a wok over medium-high heat.
  4. Add chicken and cook for 4 minutes, stirring frequently.
  5. Add broccoli and water chestnuts.
  6. Add chicken pieces and toss to coat.
  7. Cook for 3 minutes, stirring frequently.
  8. Stir in honey mixture. Cook until sauce has thickened, 3 to 4 minutes.
  9. Turn heat off and stir in vinegar.
  10. Serve over rice or in lettuce wraps.
